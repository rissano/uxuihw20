Planify Travel App

Planify was designed to streamline the trip planning process for busy individuals by offering tools to organize every aspect of their travels, ensuring users can plan with ease and confidence.

Planify is a personalized travel planning app designed to help busy individuals effortlessly organize and book their trips. By leveraging AI technology, Planify creates customized itineraries based on user preferences, ensuring a seamless and stress-free travel experience. Whether it's selecting destinations, finding accommodations, or planning activities, Planify takes care of all the details, allowing users to focus on enjoying their journey.

To ensure that Planify meets the needs of its users, extensive research was conducted involving user interviews, surveys, and competitor analysis. The primary goal was to understand the pain points and preferences of individuals when planning their travels. Key insights revealed that users often struggle with the overwhelming number of options and the time-consuming nature of trip planning. They expressed a desire for a streamlined process that offers personalized recommendations and simplifies the overall planning experience. This research informed the development of Planify, guiding the design of features aimed at addressing these specific user needs and enhancing the overall user experience.

Through extensive research, it became evident that busy individuals face significant challenges when planning their trips. These challenges include:

  • Overwhelming Options: Users are often overwhelmed by the sheer number of choices available for destinations, accommodations, and activities.
  • Time-Consuming Planning: The process of organizing a trip is time-consuming and stressful, making it difficult for users to balance with their work and personal lives.
  • Lack of Personalized Recommendations: Users struggle to find travel options tailored to their specific preferences and needs, leading to less satisfactory travel experiences.

The prototyping phase for Planify involved creating low-fidelity and high-fidelity wireframes to design the app's interface and functionality. Initial sketches outlined the basic structure, which were then developed into digital wireframes. User feedback from usability testing led to several iterations, refining the design to ensure an intuitive and user-friendly experience. The high-fidelity prototypes closely resemble the final product, highlighting key features like personalized trip recommendations and a streamlined booking process.

During the testing phase, Planify was evaluated by 5 users to identify areas for improvement. Key tasks included creating an account, setting destination preferences, and navigating the explore page. Feedback highlighted the need for better visibility of tips and specific dates for trip recommendations. Iterations focused on enhancing user experience by addressing these issues, resulting in a more intuitive and functional app.

UX Case Study - Problem Section

User Insight Statement

Ava, who is very busy with her work responsibilities, needs assistance in planning her trips because she wants to spend less time planning.

During user interviews, I discovered that the majority of the users' jobs are very time demanding. When it comes to booking a trip, they find planning very stressful. Too many dissatisfactory options make users feel overwhelmed.

Therefore, I believe that people need assistance planning trips tailored around their needs. By developing an app that could make it easier for them to plan a trip, I may be able to help.

Problem Statement

Through my extensive research, I’ve observed that people with busy work schedules have difficulty finding time to plan the perfect trip. They feel it takes a significant amount of time to plan every aspect, resulting in inadequate planning, missed opportunities, increased stress, and budgeting challenges. How might we assist busy workers, who have limited time outside work, in booking trips tailored to their needs in an efficient manner?

Ideation

  • I like being able to walk to nearby attractions from my hotel.
  • I wish I could get recommendations based on my interests and previous travels.
  • What if an app could plan out the best vacation for me?

Feature Prioritization

  2. A whole itinerary booked before my trip.

Testing and Iterations

User Testing Plan

Overview

I conducted testing with 5 users to understand how they use the app, and to identify any changes that need to be made to improve the app's functionality.

Objectives

  • Can users create an account?
  • Can users make a destination preference?
  • Can users navigate to the explore page to view their trip recommendations?

Tasks

  1. Create an account to view the home screen.
  2. Save a destination in the preference settings.
  3. View the explore page to see trip recommendations.

Feedback

  • Users felt the app was very easy to navigate.
  • Users liked the integration of AI to organize and plan customized trips.
  • Users felt the tips on the home screen weren’t viewable long enough to read.
  • Users wanted to see specific dates in the explore page so they’d know what weather to expect and the opportunity to change the date.

Prioritization Matrix

User Flow Iterations

The main issues I iterated in my user flow after testing:

  1. Users unsure if their destination preference saved.
  2. Users wanting to see dates and weather for those dates next to trip recommendations.
